Mr. Osudar

Posted on 4/25/2018

Today we interviewed Mr. Osudar and asked him questions 1, 2, 4, 5, 6, and 8.

When asked Question 1 Mr.O said, "I thought when I was doing my Computer Science course the skills learned
would be used to provide a learning workflow. Mr.O also said he was hoping to become a game developer".

When asked Question 2 Mr.O responded with, "I apply my computer skills to my ability to learn, this provides
me more workflow. I sometimes feels like I lacks the skills I need. I feels that the computer helps him distribute workflow to others".

When asked Question 4 Mr.O said, " I think that computer science is practical mathematics. I also think that computer
computer science is the optimal way for someone to learn about the world".

When asked Question 5 Mr.O said, "I took a grade 11 course at St Patrick's HS, I was learning visual basics and was
making the game jeopardy to learn about if statements. I started with math and cs courses in university. I didn't
enjoy it at the time. I never had a "job" in computer science but I was interested. overall I wanted something to do".

When asked Question 6 Mr.O responded with, "My teachers throughout school and Daniel Shiffman influenced my opinions of
computer science".
